  <section class="firstaid-section">
    <div class="container">
      <h2 class="section-title">Life-Saving Techniques</h2>
      <div class="accordion">
        <div class="accordion-item">
          <button class="accordion-header">❤️ CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)</button>
          <div class="accordion-body">
            <ol>
              <li>Check responsiveness — tap the person and shout.</li>
              <li>Call emergency services immediately.</li>
              <li>Place the person on their back on a firm surface.</li>
              <li>Push hard and fast in the center of the chest — 100–120 compressions per minute.</li>
              <li>If trained, give 2 rescue breaths after every 30 compressions.</li>
            </ol>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="accordion-item">
          <button class="accordion-header">🩸 Treating Severe Bleeding</button>
          <div class="accordion-body">
            <ol>
              <li>Apply firm, direct pressure with a clean cloth.</li>
              <li>Elevate the injured area above heart level if possible.</li>
              <li>Do NOT remove the cloth — add more layers if needed.</li>
              <li>Seek emergency medical help immediately.</li>
            </ol>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="accordion-item">
          <button class="accordion-header">🔥 Burns Treatment</button>
          <div class="accordion-body">
            <ol>
              <li>Cool the burn under cool running water for 10–20 minutes.</li>
              <li>Remove jewelry or tight items near the burn.</li>
              <li>Cover loosely with a sterile, non-fluffy dressing.</li>
              <li>Do NOT apply ice, butter, or toothpaste.</li>
            </ol>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="accordion-item">
          <button class="accordion-header">🤧 Choking (Heimlich Maneuver)</button>
          <div class="accordion-body">
            <ol>
              <li>Stand behind the person and wrap your arms around their waist.</li>
              <li>Make a fist with one hand, place it above the navel.</li>
              <li>Grasp your fist with the other hand and thrust inward and upward.</li>
              <li>Repeat until the object is expelled or help arrives.</li>
            </ol>
          </div>
        </div>
        <div class="accordion-item">
          <button class="accordion-header">🦴 Fractures & Sprains</button>
          <div class="accordion-body">
            <ol>
              <li>Do NOT move the injured limb.</li>
              <li>Immobilize it with a splint or sling if available.</li>
              <li>Apply ice wrapped in cloth to reduce swelling.</li>
              <li>Seek professional medical attention.</li>
            </ol>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
